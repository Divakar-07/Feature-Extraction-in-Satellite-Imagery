Dataset Overview

The GeoVision dataset is based on high-resolution satellite imagery of Dubai, annotated with six semantic land cover classes. Each class is represented using a unique color and polygon mask to aid in training segmentation models.

Dubai Data Set:
Water Land Road Building Vegetation Unlabeled
Test Data Set:
Water Land Road Building Vegetation Unlabeled
